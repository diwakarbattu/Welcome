Stock information at your business location

Product-wise inventory at your fingertips, neoInt gives you detail information about the stock with multiple filters.

  • Go to the home page.
  • View your inventory by ‘near expiry’,‘low stock’ and to sort with more options tap on.
  • Tap on product image to view pack- wise inventory.
  • Tap Filter icon for more filters.

How can I add goods received from warehouse to my inventory?

You will receive an app notification with detail information on received shipper and product packs. As a user you can confirm the goods receipt or capture the product details manually to add in to your inventory.

  • On home page, you will receive a notification on stock receipt icon
  • Tap and select stock receipt out the list of stock receipts.
  • Capture cross check the received inventory and receipt details.
  • Click on accept button to add the products to the inventory.
  • You can scan mPins or capture the product details manually and add them to the inventory.
